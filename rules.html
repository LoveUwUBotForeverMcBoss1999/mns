Rules • MNS Minecraft Network

General Rules

  • Be respectful: Treat all players and staff with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.
  • No cheating: Mods, hacks, or exploits that give unfair advantages are strictly prohibited.
  • No griefing: Only interact with other players' builds or belongings with permission.
  • Keep chat clean: Avoid spam, excessive caps, or offensive language.
  • No advertising: Do not advertise other servers or websites.
  • Follow staff directions: The staff's decisions are final. Disputes can be addressed via official channels.

Survival Rules

  • No griefing or stealing: Respect other players' builds and belongings.
  • PvP rules: PvP is allowed only in designated areas or if both parties consent.
  • Build responsibly: Avoid building near spawn or too close to others without permission.
  • No exploiting bugs: Report bugs to the staff instead of using them to gain advantages.
  • Environment-friendly: Avoid destroying landscapes unnecessarily.

SkyBlock Rules

  • Island rules: Each player or team is limited to one island.
  • No island griefing: Respect your teammates and neighbors.
  • Trade fairly: Scamming in trades is not allowed.
  • No AFK farming: Using external tools or exploits to farm resources while AFK is prohibited.
  • Respect the economy: Avoid exploiting the shop system or game economy.

SkyWars Rules

  • No teaming: SkyWars is a solo or team-based game; do not team with opponents.
  • No camping: Avoid staying in one spot to delay the game.
  • Play fair: No hacks, cheats, or unfair mods to gain advantages.
  • Sportsmanship: Be respectful to other players, win or lose.
  • No exploiting: Do not use glitches to bypass game mechanics.

LifeSteal Rules

  • No spawn-killing: Avoid repeatedly killing players near spawn.
  • Keep PvP fair: No excessive teaming against solo players.
  • Stealing lives responsibly: Stealing lives through PvP is part of the game, but intentional griefing is not allowed.
  • No unfair advantages: No using hacked clients or exploit mods to win fights.
  • No life boosting: Do not trade lives in unfair ways to gain an advantage.

Discord Server Rules

  • Be respectful: Treat everyone in the Discord with kindness and respect.
  • Appropriate content only: No NSFW, offensive, or overly political content.
  • No spam: Avoid spamming text, images, or pings.
  • Use channels correctly: Keep discussions relevant to the designated channels.
  • No advertising: Do not post links or advertisements without permission.
  • Follow Discord TOS: Ensure your behavior aligns with Discord's Terms of Service.
  • Respect staff: Listen to moderators and administrators.
